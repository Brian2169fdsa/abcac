IC&RC Reciprocity Request

Note:
As an IC&RC member board, ABCAC facilitates reciprocity transfers to and from 78 member boards across 48 U.S. states/territories and 16 international regions. Processing fee: $150 paid to IC&RC (credit card only).
Transfer OUT of Arizona

Request to transfer your ABCAC credential to another IC&RC member board.

Transfer INTO Arizona

If you hold an IC&RC credential from another state, contact your current board to initiate the reciprocity process. They will send your application to IC&RC, who will forward it to ABCAC.

Info
Contact your current board first. ABCAC will reach out to you once we receive your approved application from IC&RC.
IC&RC International Certificate

If you hold a reciprocal-level ABCAC credential, you may order an IC&RC International Certificate — a globally recognized endorsement of your certification.
