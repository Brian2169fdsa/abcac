The International Certification & Reciprocity Consortium (IC&RC)
is the global leader in credentialing for prevention, addiction treatment, and recovery professionals. Representing over 78 certification boards and 50,000 professionals worldwide, IC&RC sets internationally recognized standards through evidence-based exams and reciprocal credentials that promote public protection and professional excellence.

Request an IC&RC International Certificate
If you're currently certified by ABCAC at the reciprocal level, you may be eligible to receive an internationally recognized credential through the
International Certification & Reciprocity Consortium (IC&RC).
What Is an International Certificate?
The IC&RC International Certificate is a globally recognized credential that mirrors your ABCAC-issued certification. It is not a standalone certification, but rather an extension of your current license or credential through an IC&RC Member Board.
Your International Certificate will carry the same
expiration date
as your existing ABCAC credential.
How to Apply
To request an IC&RC International Certificate:
Confirm your eligibility
with ABCAC.
You must hold a valid, reciprocal-level certification (e.g., CADAC, AADC, CCS, CPS, CCJP, CPRS).
Gather your current ABCAC certificate
.
You will need to upload this as proof of eligibility.
Complete the official IC&RC Order Form
.
You will choose your certification type and submit a $30 fee for each certificate requested.
Available International Certificates
ICADC – Internationally Certified Alcohol and Drug Counselor
ICAADC – Internationally Certified Advanced Alcohol and Drug Counselor
ICCS – Internationally Certified Clinical Supervisor
ICPS – Internationally Certified Prevention Specialist
ICCJP – Internationally Certified Criminal Justice Professional
ICPR – Internationally Certified Peer Recovery
ICPR-A – Internationally Certified Peer Recovery – Associate
