Transferring
to
Arizona
To transfer your IC&RC credential to Arizona:
You must already hold an IC&RC-recognized credential
from your current certifying board.
Initiate the transfer
with your current board — they will contact ABCAC to begin the reciprocity process.
ABCAC will review your credential and notify you once your transfer has been approved.
If it's been more than 4 weeks
since initiating the transfer and you haven't received updates, please contact both your original board and IC&RC for assistance.
Fee:
$150 (due upon approval)

Transferring
from
Arizona
If you currently hold a credential with ABCAC and want to transfer to another IC&RC board:
You must hold an
IC&RC credential issued by ABCAC
.
ABCAC-issued IC&RC credentials include:
Certified Alcohol and Drug Abuse Counselor (CADAC)
Advanced Alcohol and Drug Counselor (AADC)
Certified Prevention Specialist (CPS)
Certified Peer Recovery Specialist (CPRS)
Certified Criminal Justice Professional (CCJP)
Certified Clinical Supervisor (CCS)
