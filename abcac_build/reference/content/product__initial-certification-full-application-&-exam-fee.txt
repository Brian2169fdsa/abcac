TITLE: Initial Certification – Full Application & Exam Fee
META: Apply for Your Initial Certification through the Arizona Board for the Certification of Addiction Counselors (ABCAC)
This payment covers the full certification process for first-time applicants, including:•Application Manuals (General & Supplemental, as applicable)•Application Processing•IC&RC Exam Fee (for applicable credentials)•Two Years of Certification (once approved)•In-Person Testing Option – Take your IC&RC exam at a certified testing center in Arizona (Phoenix or Flagstaff). Testing centers provide a secure and professionally proctored environment.


This option applies to all of the following ABCAC credentials:

•CAC – Certified Addiction Counselor•CADAC – Certified Alcohol & Drug Abuse Counselor•AADC – Advanced Alcohol & Drug Counselor•CCS – Certified Clinical Supervisor•CPRS – Certified Peer Recovery Specialist•CCJP – Certified Criminal Justice Addictions Professional•CPS – Certified Prevention Specialist


Each credential has unique education, experience, and exam requirements. The IC&RC exam type varies by credential (ADC, PR, CCS, PS, CCJP), but all are available for in-person delivery.


 For questions or assistance, contact us at abcac@abcac.org or call 480-980-1770.
