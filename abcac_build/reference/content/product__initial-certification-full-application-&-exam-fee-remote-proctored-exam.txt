TITLE: Initial Certification – Full Application & Exam Fee (Remote Proctored Exam)
META: Apply for Your Initial Certification through the Arizona Board for the Certification of Addiction Counselors (ABCAC)
This payment covers the full certification process for first-time applicants, including:•Application Manuals (General & Supplemental, as applicable)•Application Processing•IC&RC Exam Fee (for applicable credentials)•Two Years of Certification (once approved)•Remote-Proctored Exam Option – Take your IC&RC exam from a secure, approved location with online proctoring for added flexibility and convenience (available for most credentials).
This option applies to all of the following ABCAC credentials:•CAC – Certified Addiction Counselor•CADAC – Certified Alcohol & Drug Abuse Counselor•AADC – Advanced Alcohol & Drug Counselor•CCS – Certified Clinical Supervisor•CPRS – Certified Peer Recovery Specialist•CCJP – Certified Criminal Justice Addictions Professional•CPS – Certified Prevention Specialist


Each credential has its own education and experience requirements. The IC&RC exam type may differ by credential (e.g., ADC, PR, CCS, PS, CCJP), but remote proctoring is available for all IC&RC exams offered by ABCAC.


 For questions or help selecting the right credential, contact us at abcac@abcac.org or call 480-980-1770.
