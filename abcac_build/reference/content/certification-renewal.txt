How often do I need to renew my certification?
ABCAC certifications must be renewed every two years to remain valid. Your renewal date is based on the date of your last certification approval.
What is the renewal fee?
The renewal fee for all ABCAC certifications is $150, which covers application processing and two additional years of certification.
What are requirements for certification renewal?
The requirements for certification renewal with the Arizona Board for the Certification of Addiction Counselors (ABCAC) are as follows:
✅ General Renewal Requirements (All Credentials):
Submit the Recertification Application – Complete the appropriate renewal packet for your credential.
Pay the $150 Renewal Fee – Covers application processing and two more years of certification.
Complete 40 Hours of Continuing Education (CEUs):
Must be related to substance abuse and/or behavioral health.
3 hours in Ethics (required)
3 hours in Cultural Diversity (required)
Up to 20 hours may be in-service training, if verified by your supervisor.
Remain in Good Ethical Standing – No disciplinary actions or violations of the ABCAC Code of Ethics during the current certification period.
📌 Additional CEU Requirements by Credential:
Credential	Additional Required CEUs
CCS (Clinical Supervisor)	6 hours in Clinical Supervision (in addition to 3 in Ethics + 3 in Cultural Diversity)
CPRS (Peer Recovery Specialist)	6 hours in Ethics/Professional Responsibility + 3 hours in Cultural Diversity (from the 20-hour total)
📩 Questions? Contact abcac@abcac.org or call 480-980-1770. Let me know if you’d like this in checklist or printable format!
What documents do I need for renewal?
To renew your certification with the Arizona Board for the Certification of Addiction Counselors (ABCAC), you will need to submit the following documents:
✅ Required Documents for Certification Renewal:
Completed Recertification Application
Use the correct renewal packet for your credential (e.g., CAC, CADAC, AADC, CCS, CPRS, etc.).
Continuing Education Documentation
Proof of 40 hours of continuing education (CEUs).
Must include:
3 hours in Ethics
3 hours in Cultural Diversity
(For CCS only): 6 hours in Clinical Supervision
Certificates, transcripts, or provider letters must be attached.
Documentation of In-Service Training (if applicable)
Up to 20 of the 40 hours may be from in-service training.
Must be signed and verified by your supervisor.
Included in the recertification packet.
$150 Renewal Fee
Payable to ABCAC by check, money order, or online (if available).
📩 Submit documents preferably through the form submission process for faster processing, or email them to abcac@abcac.org.
Questions? Call 480-980-1770. Let me know if you'd like a printable checklist or digital form link!
What if my certification expires before I renew?
If your certification has lapsed, you may still renew—but additional documentation or late processing may apply. Contact abcac@abcac.org for instructions based on your specific situation.
Where do I submit my renewal materials?
Submit documents by email to abcac@abcac.org or mail to:
